Become a Sponsor

PostgreSQL Conference Europe is a perfect opportunity for companies to reach out to users and developers of PostgreSQL, or to just give back to the community.

Our sponsorship program covers a wide range of levels, from cheap to big scale. Outside of these levels, we are always interested in discussing options if you have an idea for a specific way you would like to sponsor the conference. If you are interested in sponsoring the conference, please let us know!

  • Logo on website
  • One free pass

  • Logo on website
  • Logo on t-shirt
  • Two free passes

Gold 4750 €

  • .. and more

  • Logo on website

  • Four free passes
  • ..and more

Platinum Sold Out

As a Platinum partner the Business will be entitled to the following promotional opportunities:

  • Permission for the Business to use the following endorsement: "Platinum Partner of PGConf.EU 2014"
  • Prominent inclusion of the Business' logo and a link to the Business' website on the official conference website
  • The Business' logo printed on the official PGConf.EU 2014 attendees t-shirts
  • Inclusion of three A4 or Letter sized pages of the Business' promotional material - optionally in a folder without bindings - in the conference packs for registered attendees
  • Four free attendee passes for the conference (not including admission to training sessions)
  • A desk in the exhibitors area of the conference
  • Inclusion of one or two small Business promotional giveaways in the conference packs for registered attendees
  • Selection of one 20 minute keynote talk to be presented in at the conference, title, abstract and speaker name to be agreed with PostgreSQL Europe, prior to the end date of the general Call For Papers
  • The opportunity to include corporate signage up to a size of 2 metres high by 1 metre wide at the front of one lecture rooms

Cost for Platinum Partnership opportunities, as described above, is 8500 €. Additional donations are welcome.
