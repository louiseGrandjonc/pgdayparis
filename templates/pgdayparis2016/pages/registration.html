Registration is now open!

You can for now on register to come to the pgDay Paris the 31st of March 2016. The cost is of €50 per person.

The program for this year's pgDay Paris is not yet available, but it already possible to register with the EARLYBIRD code for a 30% discount!

Registration includes access to all sessions, lunch, two catered coffee breaks and (almost) all the coffee you can drink in a day.

Register Now!

Cancellation policy:

  • If you need to cancel your registration after you have paid, please contact us for manual handling.
  • If you cancel at least 4 weeks before the conference, we will refund the full cost of the registration, minus any transaction fees.
  • If you cancel at least 2 weeks before the conference, we will refund 50% of the cost of the registration, minus any transaction fees.
  • If you cancel less than 2 weeks before the conference, we will refund 25% of the cost of the registration, minus any transaction fees.
  • If you cancel less than 1 week before the conference, we can unfortunately not provide you with a refund.
