Our sponsors

pgDay Paris offers a great chance to reach PostgreSQL professionals and community members in the Paris area. We have two levels of sponsorship opportunities available: Partner and Supporter.

If you are interested in sponsoring the conference, you can sign up directly on the site. You'll be able to get the process started for all sponsorship levels there, and for Support sponsorship you will be able to complete the whole process within minutes. Contact us at org@pgday.paris for more information. We look forward to working with you!
